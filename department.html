The machine is used during surgical procedures to provide high resolution X-Ray images in real time allowing doctor to see progress & make decision swiftly. The department well versed in loco meter system, Using surgical & nonsurgical treatment of injuries, traumas tumors soon so that it could shoulder with mission of the Hospital.

General Medicines








Department of General Medicines deals with very simple to very intricate aliments proving proper, Examination, immunization, assessment and treatment in all conditions by a team of specialized well experienced medical team. Diabetes is treated not only with medication but a thorough observation & guidelines are given related to diet & over all life style change to control and help reverse diabetes.

Department of cardiology deals with almost all kinds of disorders of heart. Parts of circulatory system. Department of Ophthalmology









This department of Panacea Hospital deals with the anatomy, physiology, and diseases of eyeball & orbit. Hospital caters a team of ophthalmologist, optometrist, other staff using both the methods medicine & surgery.

Department of ENT





Panacea Hospital has setup a separate department of ENT. The department deals all diseases related to ears, nose, nasal passage, sinuses, larynx, oral cavity, upper pharynx & structure of face & nose. The dept. has got ENT specialist having vast and diverse exposure.
