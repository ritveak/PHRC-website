About Us


Dr Girish Bansal's dream materialized with the existence of Little Scholars' Academy society when he thought Education & Medical, Even stronger pillars of strong pillar of human's healthy progress. All the members of the society have been striving for social causes uplifting marginalized people and catering education and health for the long time.
Serving a golden age in Education, It was decided to serve humanity in Medical, All this multitude accumulated & took the form of 'PANACEA HOSPITAL'.







Mission, Vision and Objective

Nothing has emerged more rapidly than commercialization in all fields even that so meanly that human health is so marginalized, causing national health crippled & unaccessable to masses, In such scenario service that of International class is out of question.
PANACEA HOSPITAL is an International class health care with cozy, glossy & hyper hygienic environment. It aims to provide medical facilities, accessible to masses in a courteous & proper communicado.

Its richness lies in its grandeur of being of very high standard yet providing splendid medical facilities with state-of-the-art equipments in quite affordable prices so that the medical facilities of PANACEA must reach to the people of all financial status & Hospital could do its Endeavour in making national health happy & proud.

To provide medical facilities and treatment in quite affordable prices so that the medical facilities of PANACEA must reach to the people of all financial status & Hospital could do its Endeavour in making national health happy & proud.
